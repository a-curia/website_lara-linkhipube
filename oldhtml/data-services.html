Why Data Services?

Data Is Your Most Valuable Asset

Every transaction and operational event contains valuable intelligence. LINKHIPU helps you capture, clean, transform, and present your data with GDPR-compliant governance.

  • Cloud-native data warehouses on Snowflake, BigQuery, and Redshift
  • ELT/ETL pipelines using dbt, Apache Spark, and Kafka
  • Executive dashboards with Power BI, Tableau, and Looker
  • GDPR-compliant data governance and quality frameworks
End-to-End Pipelines

From Ingestion to Insights

We connect your data sources through purpose-built ingestion pipelines feeding a centralised, well-governed data platform.

  • Real-time streaming with Apache Kafka and AWS Kinesis
  • Batch ingestion from databases, APIs, and flat files
  • Data quality checks and anomaly detection at every stage
  • dbt transformations — version-controlled and fully tested
